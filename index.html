HealthAloha

HealthAloha is a personalized patient engagement and care navigation tool for patients recovering at home. It help Hospitals (HOPDs) and Surgery Centers (ASCs):

  • Reduce bad pre-op prep, "no show's" & cancellations.
  • Empower and educate patient family caregivers. Decrease patient anxiety, increase adherence.
  • Enable real-time remote monitoring & alerts for Care Teams.
  • Minimize post-op complications & costly readmissions.
  • Gather and analyze patient reported outcomes for clinical, financial & regulatory measures.
